Biography

     Nicole Baster is a skilled artist who finds joy in ALL of her art work. When Nicole was a litle girl, she always found the concept of art interesting. She would make art work for her parents, take pictures of nature, create characters, etc. When Nicole was about five years old, she joined an art studio called Pricilla and Tiffany Art Corp. As she began spending more and more years at the studio, she began realizing that this was what she wanted to do from now on . Now as a thirteen year old, she has produced many eye catching pieces of art work. 
Nicole has done paintings from animals all the way to humans. She has proven to create anything you ask her to whenever and wherever you want. She takes pride in her work and loves what she does. Nicole Baster is currently attending Miami Arts Studio and is in the strand Technology Production. She is learning how to become a professional graphic artist and also learning how to use softwares such as the whole Adobe and Microsoft Suite. When Nicole gets an opportunity to learn more about graphic arts, she takes advantage of it and does the best 
she can! Nicole is thinking about making a future of her graphic artist skills and working for Pixar Animation Studios. Nicole believes that with her knowledge about art and graphic arts, she will succeed in everything she does. As an artist, she plans to inspire people everywhere she goes. Nicole always puts 100% of effort into her work and is always looking for a great challenge to conquer.

Goals and Achievements

Professional Graphic Artist

As a graphic artist, I hope to learn and be certified on all of the Adobe Suite products. Every year as I learn more and more about graphic arts, I feel self assured that I am on my way to becoming a professional graphic artist!

Art Work

Being an artist, I have many goals and aspirations towards my art work. I  hope that I can get rocognized for my work and have my art work be put in museums for many people to see. Becoming a well known artist that people admire and use for inspiration, is somebody I want to be in the near future.

Pixar Animation Studios 

Having a full time job at Pixar Studios has been one of my goals since a little girl. Making Pixar films/movies would be my dream goal that i would hope to achievein the future. I would hope to be the animator or character designer when working at Pixar Animation studios.

World Traveler

Traveling around the world, taking gorgeous pictures, and then recreating them digitally or hand painted is another goal I would also want to achieve in the near future.
